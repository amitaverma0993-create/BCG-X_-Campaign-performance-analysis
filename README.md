# BCG X Job Simulation — Campaign Performance Analysis

## Project Summary

This project was completed as part of the **Introduction to Data for Decision Makers Job Simulation** with **BCG X**.
BCG X combines consulting and advanced technology capabilities to help organizations accelerate digital transformation and growth. <br>
In this simulation, I worked as part of a cross-functional team analyzing a **multi-channel marketing campaign** for **NewCo** clint, a national retail brand focused on acquiring new customers.

## Business Objective

NewCo completed a one-week digital campaign testing:

1.	Two message styles

**Campaign A**: Informal, conversational tone <br>
**Campaign B**: Sales-focused, promotional tone

3.	Three marketing channels

**Email**  
**Instagram**  
**Website Banner**

**The client wanted to know:**

1.	Which campaign + channel combination delivered the most value? <br>  
2.	What drove engagement from new customers? <br> 
3.	Where should marketing investment be focused next quarter? <br>

## Methodology

### Step 1 – Overall Performance Analysis

1.	Reviewed total revenue by channel

2.	Compared total revenue by campaign messaging

3.	Identified highest-performing combinations

### Step 2 – New Customer Segmentation

1.	Created new pivot tables isolating new customers

2.	Used slicers to filter customer type

3.	Compared trends between:

A.	Total Sales

B.	New Customer Sales

### Step 3 – Behavioral Analysis

1.	Evaluated Time on Site for non-purchasers

2.	Interpreted engagement as future retargeting opportunity

## Key Insights

1.	Email emerged as a strong revenue-driving channel.

2.	Campaign messaging influenced customer response differently across channels.

3.	Email + Campaign A performed best in attracting new customers.

4.	Engagement metrics revealed additional retargeting opportunities.

5.	Strategies that maximize total sales may differ from those that maximize new customer acquisition.

## Strategic Recommendation

Based on analysis:

1.	Double down on Email for new customer growth

2.	Continue leveraging Campaign A tone for first-time buyer engagement

3.	Retarget high Time-on-Site users who did not purchase

4.	Balance short-term revenue goals with long-term customer acquisition strategy

## Key Business Tradeoff

The most important consideration before scaling strategy:

**Optimizing for total revenue vs. optimizing for new customer growth.**

A high-revenue campaign may not be the best at expanding customer base.
