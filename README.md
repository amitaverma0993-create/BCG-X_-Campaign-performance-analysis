# BCG X Job Simulation — Campaign Performance Analysis

## Project Summary

This project was completed as part of the **Introduction to Data for Decision Makers Job Simulation** with **BCG X**.
BCG X combines consulting and advanced technology capabilities to help organizations accelerate digital transformation and growth.  
In this simulation, I worked as part of a cross-functional team analyzing a **multi-channel digital marketing campaign** for **NewCo** clint, a national retail brand focused on acquiring new customers.

## Business Objective

NewCo launched a one-week marketing experiment testing in two messaging styles across three digital channels.

1. Two message styles

   **Campaign A**: Informal, conversational tone  
   **Campaign B**: Sales-focused, promotional tone  

2. Three marketing channels

     **Email**    
     **Instagram**    
     **Website Banner**  

**The client wanted to know:**

1.	Which campaign + channel combination delivered the most value?    
2.	What drove engagement from new customers?   
3.	Where should marketing investment be focused next quarter?

## Analytical Approach

### Step 1 – Overall Performance Analysis

1. Reviewed total revenue by channel  
2. Compared total revenue by campaign messaging  
3. Identified highest-performing combinations  

### Step 2 – New Customer Segmentation

1. Created new pivot tables isolating new customers  
2. Used slicers to filter customer type  
3. Compared trends between:  
A.Total Sales  
B.New Customer Sales  

### Step 3 – Behavioral Analysis

1. Evaluated Time on Site for non-purchasers  
2. Interpreted engagement as future retargeting opportunity  

## Key Insights

1. Campaign A via Email generated the highest **new customer sales: $2,124.47**  
2. Email emerged as a strong revenue-driving channel.  
3. This was followed by **Campaign A on Instagram ($969.46) and Web Banner ($633.06)**.    
4. Across all channels, Campaign A consistently outperformed Campaign B in driving new customer purchases.    
5. Despite Campaign B showing stronger total sales overall (**A = $5,869.56 compared to B = $8,790.04**).    
6. Email + Campaign A performed best in attracting new customers.      
6. Engagement metrics revealed additional retargeting opportunities.      
7. Strategies that maximize total sales may differ from those that maximize new customer acquisition.      

## Strategic Recommendation

Based on analysis:

We suggest prioritizing **Campaign A through the Email channel** for your next campaign targeting new customer growth.   
It consistently delivers the strongest results across multiple touchpoints and aligns well with your acquisition goals.  

## Key Business Tradeoff

The most important consideration before scaling strategy:  

**Optimizing for total revenue vs. optimizing for new customer growth.**  
A high-revenue campaign may not be the best at expanding customer base.
